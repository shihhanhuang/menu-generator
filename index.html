What should I select?

First place the Paprika export in the app's `imports` folder. Newest files appear first here.

Select the file, or files, that together represent the recipe library you want active in the app.

  • If your new export contains all current favorites, select only that new export.
  • If your new export is just a small batch, select it together with your main/full export.
  • Your planner attributes and tags are stored separately and should carry over when the same recipe appears again.
